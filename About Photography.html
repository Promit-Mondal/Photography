  1. What is Photography?

    "Photography is the art, application and practice of creating durable images by recording light or other electromagnetic radiation, either electronically by means of an image sensor, or chemically by means of a light-sensitive material such as photographic film."

  2. How to click better Photos?

    1. Don't move or shake your camera.Try to hold it still.
    2. To click picture at high zoom use tripod.
    3. Always focus on that subject which you want to capture.
    4. Use different camera modes according to the scene like firework,sports,landscape,portrait.
    5. Use different whitebalance according to the light of outside like daylight,flurocent,tungsten.
    6. Use manual mode when the picture quality is more important.
    7. Use other modes when the scene is more important.
